How to Reach Solapur

By Road

Solapur is well connected by highways to major cities such as Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru.

  • Pune – 250 km
  • Hyderabad – 300 km
  • Mumbai – 400 km
  • Bengaluru – 380 km

By Rail

Solapur Railway Station is a major railway junction connecting Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Bengaluru.

By Air

  • Pune International Airport
  • Hyderabad International Airport

Local Transport

  • Auto rickshaws
  • Taxis
  • City buses
